# SunGrid Cooperative — Billing & Account FAQs

## How is my monthly bill calculated?
Your bill combines a fixed cooperative membership fee with variable charges based on net metered usage.

| Tier | Monthly Membership Fee | Net Metering Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Community | $12 | $0.09/kWh |
| Household | $8 | $0.07/kWh credit |
| Commercial | $150 | Negotiated per contract |

## When are bills issued?
Bills are issued on the 1st of each month and are due within 21 days. Members enrolled in autopay are charged on the 15th.

## What happens if I miss a payment?
Accounts more than 45 days past due are flagged for a service review. SunGrid does not disconnect community-tier members from shared array access for billing disputes; disconnection policy applies only to non-payment exceeding 90 days.

## Can I update my payment method?
Yes, through the member portal under Account Settings. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

## My rebate hasn't appeared on my bill yet — who do I contact?
Rebate disbursement timing is governed by the Incentive & Rebate Programs process, not the standard billing cycle. Contact the Incentive Program committee directly rather than general billing support.

## What is the annual true-up?
Household-tier and Community-tier members on net metering undergo an annual true-up each March, reconciling any excess credits or debits accumulated over the prior 12 months. Members with excess credits at true-up receive a bill credit at the applicable net metering rate; members with a net debit are billed the difference at the standard (non-net-metered) rate for their tier.

## Can I dispute a charge on my bill?
Billing disputes not related to rebate eligibility are handled through the member grievance procedure described in the Program Policies document, and must be filed within 60 days of the disputed charge appearing on a statement.

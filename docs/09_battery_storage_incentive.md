# SunGrid Cooperative — Battery Storage Add-On Incentive

## Overview
Household-tier members who have already received a favorable eligibility determination under the Rooftop Rebate Program may apply for an additional Battery Storage Add-On Incentive when installing battery storage alongside their rooftop system.

## Incentive Structure
The Battery Storage Add-On Incentive is paid at $0.15 per watt-hour of usable storage capacity, capped at $1,500 per household. This is a separate calculation from the Rooftop Rebate Program's $0.40/watt rebate and does not count against the $4,000 Rooftop Rebate cap.

## Eligibility
- The associated rooftop system must have an approved Rooftop Rebate Program determination on file.
- Battery installation must be performed by a SunGrid-approved installer meeting the certification requirements described in the Installer Certification & Renewal document.
- Battery systems must meet the technical requirements described in the Technical & Installation Guidance materials for battery storage.

## Standalone Battery Installations
Battery storage installed without an accompanying rooftop solar system is not eligible for this incentive. Members interested in standalone battery storage should contact SunGrid support to discuss options outside the incentive program.

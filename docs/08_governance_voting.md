# SunGrid Cooperative — Governance & Voting Rights

## Voting Eligibility
Voting rights at the annual cooperative meeting are tied to membership tier. Household and Commercial-tier members receive one vote per account. Community-tier members receive one vote per account regardless of the size of their shared-array subscription.

## Board Elections
Board seats are elected by household-tier members during the spring general meeting, on a staggered schedule with roughly one-third of seats up for election each year. Candidates must have been members in good standing for at least 12 months prior to nomination.

## Proxy Voting
Members unable to attend the annual meeting in person may assign a proxy vote to another member in good standing through the member portal, no later than 5 business days before the meeting.

## Quorum Requirements
A quorum of 15% of eligible voting members (in person or by proxy) is required for any binding vote at the annual meeting. If quorum is not met, votes are deferred to a follow-up meeting held within 30 days.

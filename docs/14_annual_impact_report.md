# SunGrid Cooperative — Annual Impact Report Summary

## Membership Growth
SunGrid ended the fiscal year with just over 5,000 members across all tiers, up from approximately 3,600 the prior year, driven primarily by Household-tier growth following the Rooftop Rebate Program's expansion.

## Installed Capacity
Total installed rooftop and community-array capacity across the cooperative reached 38 megawatts by fiscal year end, roughly double the prior year's total.

## Estimated Emissions Offset
Based on regional grid emissions factors, SunGrid estimates its members' combined generation offset approximately 21,000 metric tons of CO2-equivalent emissions over the fiscal year.

## Program Participation
Roughly 60% of eligible Household-tier members who installed solar during the fiscal year also received a Rooftop Rebate Program disbursement; the remainder either did not apply, were denied under eligibility rules, or have applications still pending review.

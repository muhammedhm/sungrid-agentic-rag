# SunGrid Cooperative — Company Updates

## Q2 Cooperative Newsletter Highlights
This quarter, SunGrid crossed 4,200 active household-tier installations across the service region, with the shared community array reaching full subscription for the first time since launch.

## New Board Members
Two new members joined the Cooperative Board this quarter, both elected by household-tier members during the spring general meeting.

## Upcoming Changes
Starting next fiscal year, SunGrid plans to introduce a fourth membership tier for multi-family housing developments. Details on eligibility and rebate treatment for this tier are expected to be published closer to launch and are not yet finalized — members should not rely on this document for eligibility guidance until the formal policy is released.

## Community Array Expansion
Due to reaching full subscription, the Cooperative Board approved funding for a second shared community array, expected to begin construction next year.

## Regional Recognition
SunGrid was recognized this quarter by a regional clean-energy association for cooperative-model innovation. The recognition specifically cited the shared community array model as a replicable approach for members without suitable rooftops.

## Staffing Update
The Cooperative added two new member-support staff this quarter to reduce grievance and support-ticket response times, following member feedback collected in the prior quarter's satisfaction survey.

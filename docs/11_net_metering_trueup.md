# SunGrid Cooperative — Net Metering True-Up Details

## What Is True-Up?
Net metering true-up is the annual reconciliation of energy credits and debits accumulated by Household and Community-tier members over a 12-month cycle ending each March, as referenced briefly in the Billing & Account FAQs document.

## Calculation
During true-up, SunGrid compares total energy exported to the grid against total energy consumed over the cycle. Members with net excess generation receive a credit at their tier's net metering rate; members with net consumption above generation are billed the shortfall at the standard (non-net-metered) rate.

## Timing and Notification
True-up statements are issued within 10 business days of the March billing cycle close. Any credit or charge resulting from true-up is applied to the next regular bill rather than issued as a separate transaction.

## Mid-Year Enrollment
Members who enroll in net metering partway through the 12-month cycle undergo a prorated true-up based on their actual enrollment period, not a full 12-month calculation.

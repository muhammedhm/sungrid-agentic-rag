# SunGrid Cooperative — Member Program Policies

## Membership Tiers
SunGrid Cooperative offers three membership tiers: Community, Household, and Commercial. Each tier determines voting rights at the annual cooperative meeting and access to shared solar capacity.

- **Community tier**: For members who do not own a rooftop suitable for panels. Members buy a share of output from a shared community array.
- **Household tier**: For members installing rooftop solar on a primary residence.
- **Commercial tier**: For member businesses installing on-site generation above 25kW.

## Code of Conduct for Installers
All installers contracted through SunGrid must hold a valid state electrical license and complete SunGrid's annual safety certification. Installers found in violation of safety protocol on two or more jobs in a 12-month period are removed from the approved installer list.

## Grievance Procedure
Members may file a grievance regarding service quality, billing disputes, or installer conduct through the member portal. Grievances are reviewed by the Cooperative Board within 15 business days. This policy does not cover disputes about rebate eligibility, which are handled under the Incentive Program appeals process (see Incentive & Rebate Programs document).

## Data Privacy
Member energy usage data is retained for 24 months and is not shared with third parties except as required for rebate program verification (see Incentive & Rebate Programs document) or by law.

## Tier Transfers
Members may request a tier change (e.g., Community to Household, upon installing a qualifying rooftop system) once per 12-month period. Tier changes take effect on the first day of the next billing cycle and do not retroactively affect rebate eligibility for systems installed under a prior tier.

## Annual Meeting Attendance
All members in good standing may attend the annual cooperative meeting regardless of tier, though voting weight and eligibility to hold Board positions vary by tier as described in the Cooperative Governance & Voting Rights document. Meeting notices are sent at least 30 days in advance via the member portal and registered email.

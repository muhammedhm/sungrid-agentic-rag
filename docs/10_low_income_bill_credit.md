# SunGrid Cooperative — Low-Income Bill Credit Program

## Overview
The Low-Income Bill Credit Program provides a recurring monthly credit to qualifying members' bills, rather than a one-time rebate. It is administered independently of the Rooftop Rebate Program and does not require the member to have solar installed.

## Eligibility
Members qualify based on household income falling below 200% of the regional low-income threshold, verified annually. Eligibility applies to all membership tiers, including Community-tier members without rooftop solar.

## Credit Amount
Qualifying households receive a flat $25 monthly credit applied automatically to their bill. The credit appears as a separate line item and does not affect net metering calculations.

## Recertification
Members must recertify income eligibility annually. Failure to recertify results in the credit being paused (not permanently revoked) until recertification is completed; paused credits are not retroactively reinstated for months missed during the lapse.

# SunGrid Cooperative — Autopay Failure & Retry Policy

## What Happens When Autopay Fails?
If a scheduled autopay charge fails (e.g., insufficient funds, expired card), SunGrid retries the charge twice more over the following 5 business days before treating the account as manual-pay for that billing cycle.

## Notifications
Members are notified by email and in the member portal after the first failed attempt, with instructions to update their payment method. A second notification is sent if the retry also fails.

## Relationship to Late Payment Policy
An account that reverts to manual-pay status due to autopay failure is still subject to the standard 45-day and 90-day late-payment timelines described in the Billing & Account FAQs document, calculated from the original due date, not from the date autopay failed.

## Re-Enrolling in Autopay
Members can re-enroll in autopay at any time through the member portal under Account Settings. Re-enrollment takes effect on the next billing cycle, consistent with standard payment method change timing.

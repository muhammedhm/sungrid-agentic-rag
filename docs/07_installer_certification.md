# SunGrid Cooperative — Installer Certification & Renewal

## Certification Requirements
Installers must hold a valid state electrical license and complete SunGrid's safety certification course before being added to the approved installer list. Certification is specific to the installer license holder, not the installing company as a whole — each individual electrician working on SunGrid jobs must be separately certified.

## Annual Renewal
Safety certification must be renewed annually. Installers who allow certification to lapse are automatically removed from the approved installer list until renewal is complete; any jobs completed during a lapsed period are treated as performed by a non-approved installer for rebate eligibility purposes, regardless of the installer's certification history before the lapse.

## Renewal Process
Renewal requires a short refresher course and a passing score on a safety knowledge assessment. SunGrid sends renewal reminders 60 and 30 days before an installer's certification expires.

## Relationship to Safety Violations
Certification renewal is separate from the safety-violation removal process described in the Program Policies document's Code of Conduct section. An installer can be in good renewal standing and still be removed from the approved list for repeated safety violations.
